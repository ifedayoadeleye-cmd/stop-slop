# Changelog

## 2026-05-03

### Added

**Phrases (references/phrases.md)**
- Complexity Signalling: phrases that announce nuance instead of demonstrating it ("This is more complex than it appears," "There are no easy answers here," "The reality is more nuanced")
- Empathy Performance: emotional reassurance pasted in without being earned by content ("This can feel overwhelming," "You're not alone in this," "Give yourself grace")
- Discovery Narration: AI performing the process of research rather than reporting findings ("As I explored this further," "What I found surprised me," "The more I looked")
- Hedged Urgency: modal hedges used to inflate importance while maintaining deniability ("This may be one of the most important," "Potentially transformative," "What might be a turning point")
- Transition Glue: connective phrases that fake logical progression between paragraphs that don't connect ("With that in mind," "Against this backdrop," "Taken together," "Zooming out," "Building on this")

**Structures (references/structures.md)**
- Enumerated Non-Lists: turning continuous argument into numbered or bulleted items where prose would serve better; rule against false frameworks
- Weak Verb Constructions: weak verb plus abstract noun instead of the strong verb directly ("work to ensure," "seek to address," "take steps to," "begin to understand")
- The Wisdom Sandwich: aphorism-opener, middle content, aphorism-closer pattern that performs rather than argues; rule for checking both ends of every paragraph
- The Summary That Restates: closing paragraphs that paraphrase the opening under "Ultimately," "In the end," or "Taken together"; rule that the final paragraph must add something

**Examples (references/examples.md)**
- Nine new before/after examples covering all new pattern categories (Examples 6-14)

**SKILL.md**
- Quick Checks expanded with nine new checks covering all new pattern categories
- Scoring table updated with sixth dimension: Structure (prose moves as argument, not list of announcements)
- Passing threshold updated from 35/50 to 42/60
